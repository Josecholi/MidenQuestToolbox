Tier Chance Finder - Tier Chance Finder
Find the T% for your tile by looking at the resources you gather.

How To Use:

  1. Drag the bookmarklet code to your bookmarks bar.
  2. Navigate to the Resource Gathering page in MQO.
  3. Click the bookmarklet.

WARNING: Navigating away from the resource gathering page will break the script.

Work Efficiency Calculator Work Efficiency Calculator
Using the Work Efficiency on your gear, determine your amount of resources found.

How To Use:

  1. Drag the bookmarklet code to your bookmarks bar.
  2. Navigate to the Resource Gathering page in MQO.
  3. Click the bookmarklet.
  4. Enter your Work Efficiency in the popup.
  5. On level up, the script will update your resources.
